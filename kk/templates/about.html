About Khovayel Khel

Started by our seniors (Team 5D) as a college project in 2016, Khovayel Khel now has become a tradition of GEC, Gandhinagar. To give GECians a break from their hectic college life and to bring the real GullyBoy out from the students, we- The IT Department Students organize Khovayel Khel every year, in which student play their favourite childhood games such as Pithu ,Rumal Khech and many more. And not only students but our respected Professors and Principal madam also takes part in this event and play with their collegues and feel nostalgic.

Last 4 Seasons have been very successful and it wouldn't have been without your support and enthusiasm. And we are hoping for the same in this upcoming 5th season.
So brace yourselves and come prepared for some lightweight competition and for alot of fun memories.
